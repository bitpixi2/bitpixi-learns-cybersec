BITPIXI LEARNS CYBERSEC: AUTHORISED TESTING POLICY
Last reviewed: 2026-07-18

PURPOSE
This is a personal public learning project with a small learning-design Easter egg.
Good-faith research is welcome only within the boundary below.

IN SCOPE
- The exact host: bitpixi-learns-cybersec.bitpixi.chatgpt.site
- Public pages, response headers and static assets on that host
- Fictional files under /field-notes/
- Low-rate, non-destructive requests that do not affect other visitors

OUT OF SCOPE
- chatgpt.site, Cloudflare, GitHub, hosting control planes and adjacent tenants
- Linked course providers, social platforms and any other third-party origin
- Credential attacks, social engineering or access to another person's data
- Denial-of-service, load, resource-exhaustion and destructive testing
- Persisting, changing or deleting data or content

FICTIONAL FIELD NOTE
#8377 and #2821 are characters patching a fictional study plan. There is no
submission endpoint, no answer needs to be hidden, and this application does
not collect answers.

REPORTING
Email Kasey.bitpixi@gmail.com with subject "BLC security report". Include the
affected URL, reproducible steps, observed impact and suggested remediation.
I aim to acknowledge useful reports within seven days. No paid bounty is
offered.

SAFE HARBOUR
Good-faith research that remains within this policy will be treated as
authorised for this project. This policy cannot authorise testing of systems
owned or operated by third parties. If uncertain, pause and ask first.
